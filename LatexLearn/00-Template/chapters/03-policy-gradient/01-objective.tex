\section{目标函数}

参数化策略记为 $\policy_{\params}(a\mid s)$。优化目标：
\[
  J(\params)=\E_{\trajectory\sim\policy_{\params}}
  \left[
    \sum_{t=0}^{T}\discount^t r_t
  \right].
\]

策略梯度定理给出：
\[
  \nabla_{\params}J(\params)
  =
  \E_{\trajectory\sim\policy_{\params}}
  \left[
    \sum_{t=0}^{T}
    \nabla_{\params}\log\policy_{\params}(a_t\mid s_t)G_t
  \right].
\]
